<p><p>The Switch Type Electrohydraulic Actuator market is divided into two main types: linear actuators and rotary actuators. Linear actuators facilitate straight-line motion, commonly used in applications requiring precise linear movement, such as valve positioning and automation processes. In contrast, rotary actuators convert hydraulic energy into rotational motion, ideal for tasks like opening and closing valves or driving mechanical components. Both actuator types are crucial in industrial automation, providing efficiency and precision in controlling various systems and operations.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Mountain Bicycles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rigid</li><li>Hardtail</li><li>Softail</li><li>Full Suspension</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mountain bicycles are categorized into four main types based on suspension designs. Rigid bikes have no suspension, providing simplicity and efficiency on smooth trails. Hardtail bikes feature a front suspension but a rigid rear, balancing performance and weight for varied terrains. Softail bikes incorporate a limited rear suspension for enhanced comfort while maintaining a lightweight frame. Full suspension bikes have both front and rear suspension, offering superior shock absorption and control, making them ideal for rugged trails and steep descents.</p></p>

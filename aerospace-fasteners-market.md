<p><strong>The Aerospace Fasteners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ThreadedFasteners</li><li>Non-ThreadedFasteners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aerospace fasteners market is primarily divided into two types: threaded and non-threaded fasteners. Threaded fasteners, such as bolts, screws, and nuts, are designed to provide strong connections through a helical ridge, ensuring reliability in high-stress environments. Non-threaded fasteners, including rivets and pins, offer seamless connections without threads, making them ideal for applications where weight reduction and aerodynamic efficiency are critical. Both types play essential roles in aircraft assembly, ensuring safety, performance, and structural integrity in aerospace applications.</p></p>

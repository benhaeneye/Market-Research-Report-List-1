<p><p>The orthopedic prosthetic devices market encompasses various segments designed to aid individuals with limb loss. Upper extremity prosthetics focus on artificial arms and hands, enhancing functionality and appearance. Lower extremity prosthetics include artificial legs and feet, facilitating mobility. Liners provide cushioning and comfort between the residual limb and the prosthetic device. Sockets are custom-fitted interfaces supporting the limb within the prosthetic. Modular components allow customization and flexibility in prosthetic design, enabling tailored solutions for users' specific needs and activities.</p></p>

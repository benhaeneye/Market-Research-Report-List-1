<p><p>The Metallocene EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer) market is segmented into two main types based on the polymerization process: solution polymerization and suspension polymerization. </p><p>Solution polymerization involves dissolving the monomers in a solvent, allowing for precise control over molecular weight and structure, resulting in enhanced material properties. Conversely, suspension polymerization involves dispersing the monomers in a liquid medium, leading to larger, more robust beads of polymer with favorable processing characteristics. Both methods cater to different applications and performance requirements within the EPDM market.</p></p>

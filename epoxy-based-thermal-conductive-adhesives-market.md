High thermal conductivity epoxy adhesives are designed for applications requiring superior heat dissipation, often used in electronics and automotive industries. Medium thermal conductivity adhesives strike a balance, suitable for general applications needing decent thermal management. Low thermal conductivity epoxy adhesives are used where thermal performance is less critical, typically in structural bonding where mechanical strength is prioritized over thermal properties.</p></p>

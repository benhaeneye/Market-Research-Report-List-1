<p><p>The fly fishing lines market features three primary types: floating lines, sink lines, and sink tip floating lines. Floating lines are designed to stay on the surface, making them ideal for dry fly fishing. Sink lines, on the other hand, are used for deeper water fishing, allowing lures to reach the bottom effectively. Sink tip floating lines combine both features, with a floating section and a sinking tip. This versatility enables anglers to adapt to various fishing conditions and depths with ease.</p></p>

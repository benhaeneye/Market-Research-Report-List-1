<p><p>The Styrene-Butadiene Rubber (SBR) based adhesive market is primarily categorized into two types: solution types and emulsion types. Solution-based adhesives are typically formulated with SBR dissolved in organic solvents, providing excellent adhesion and fast drying properties, ideal for industrial applications. In contrast, emulsion types consist of SBR particles dispersed in water, offering a safer and more environmentally friendly option with easier cleanup. Both types cater to diverse applications in automotive, construction, and packaging industries, emphasizing versatility and performance.</p></p>

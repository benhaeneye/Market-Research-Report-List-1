<p><strong>The Smart Water Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Pump</li><li>Positive Displacement Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smart water pumps market is primarily segmented into two types: centrifugal pumps and positive displacement pumps. Centrifugal pumps utilize a rotating impeller to impart kinetic energy to water, making them ideal for high-flow applications. In contrast, positive displacement pumps trap a fixed amount of water and force it through the discharge pipe, ensuring consistent flow rates, even against varying pressures. Both types play crucial roles in water management systems, enhancing efficiency, monitoring, and control in various applications.</p></p>

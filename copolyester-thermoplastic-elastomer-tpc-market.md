<p><p>The Copolyester Thermoplastic Elastomer (TPC) market comprises three key types: Injection Grade, Extrusion Grade, and Blow Molding Grade. Injection Grade TPCs are used for complex shapes and precise dimensions, making them ideal for automotive and consumer goods. Extrusion Grade TPCs are designed for continuous processes, suitable for producing films, sheets, and profiles with excellent flexibility. Blow Molding Grade TPCs cater to hollow objects like bottles and containers, offering durability and impact resistance, enhancing product performance across various industries.</p></p>

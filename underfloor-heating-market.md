<p><strong>The Underfloor Heating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydronic Heating</li><li>Electric Heating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Underfloor heating systems are primarily categorized into two types: hydronic and electric heating. Hydronic heating circulates warm water through pipes embedded in the floor, providing consistent and efficient heat distribution, making it suitable for larger spaces. In contrast, electric heating employs heating cables or mats installed beneath the floor surface, offering quick heat-up times and flexibility for smaller areas or retrofits. Both systems enhance comfort, reduce energy costs, and can be integrated with various flooring materials.</p></p>

<p><p>A Distributed Feedback Semiconductor Laser (DFB laser) is a type of laser that utilizes a periodic optical structure to achieve wavelength-selective feedback. This design allows for stable single-frequency operation, making DFB lasers ideal for applications in telecommunications, sensing, and data transmission.

<p><p>The antibody humanization market includes monoclonal antibodies, bispecific antibodies, and CDR-grafted antibodies. Monoclonal antibodies are designed to target specific antigens and are widely used in therapies. Bispecific antibodies can simultaneously bind two different antigens, enhancing therapeutic effectiveness. CDR-grafted antibodies involve embedding complementary-determining regions from non-human antibodies into a human framework to improve efficacy and reduce immunogenicity. Together, these technologies drive advancements in targeted therapies, particularly in oncology and autoimmune diseases.</p></p>

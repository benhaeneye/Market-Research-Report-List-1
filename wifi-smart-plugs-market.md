<p><p>The WiFi smart plugs market can be categorized into three main types: two-hole plugs, three-hole plugs, and porous plugs. Two-hole plugs are designed for devices with simple on/off functionality, ideal for light appliances. Three-hole plugs accommodate grounded devices, providing added safety and versatility for heavier machinery. Porous plugs, featuring multiple outlet points, allow for multiple devices to connect simultaneously, enhancing convenience for users with several smart devices. Each type caters to different user needs and electrical safety standards.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Rapid Thermal Processing Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lamp-based</li><li>Laser-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rapid Thermal Processing (RTP) machine market is primarily divided into two types: lamp-based and laser-based systems. Lamp-based RTP uses high-intensity halogen or xenon lamps to quickly heat substrates, providing uniform temperature distribution for efficient thermal treatments. In contrast, laser-based RTP employs focused laser beams to achieve precise heating, offering excellent control over temperature variations and short processing times. Both technologies cater to the semiconductor and materials industries, enhancing manufacturing efficiency and product quality.</p></p>

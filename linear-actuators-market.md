<p><p>The linear actuators market is primarily divided into three types: pneumatic, electric, and hydraulic. Pneumatic actuators use compressed air to create motion, making them ideal for applications requiring rapid actuation with less precision. Electric actuators rely on electric motors, offering precise control and energy efficiency, suitable for applications needing accurate positioning. Hydraulic actuators utilize pressurized fluid to generate high force, often used in heavy machinery where significant power is required. Each type caters to specific industrial needs and operational environments.</p></p>

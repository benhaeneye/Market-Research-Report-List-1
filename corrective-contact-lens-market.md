<p><p>The corrective contact lens market consists of three main types: rigid, soft, and hybrid lenses. Rigid contact lenses, made of durable materials, maintain their shape and provide clear vision, especially for astigmatism. Soft contact lenses, made from flexible materials, offer comfort and a wide range of prescription options, suitable for daily wear. Hybrid contact lenses combine a rigid center with a soft outer ring, providing the benefits of both types, ensuring clarity and comfort for users with varying vision needs.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Deformable Mirror Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Segmented Mirrors</li><li>Continuous Surface Mirrors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The deformable mirror market is primarily divided into two types: segmented mirrors and continuous surface mirrors. Segmented mirrors consist of multiple, individually controlled segments that can adjust their shape to correct wavefront errors, commonly used in astronomical telescopes. In contrast, continuous surface mirrors feature a single, continuous surface, allowing for smooth deformations, ideal for adaptive optics in ophthalmology and laser systems. Both types enhance imaging quality and system performance, catering to diverse applications across various industries.</p></p>

<p><p>High Molecular Weight Polyisobutylene (HMW PIB) is categorized into two main types: Conventional Polyisobutylene (C-PIB) and Highly Reactive Polyisobutylene (HR-PIB). C-PIB is widely used in adhesives, sealants, and lubricants due to its excellent tackiness and chemical stability. HR-PIB, with its enhanced reactivity, is preferred for manufacturing fuel and lubricant additives, offering improved performance in viscosity modification and deposit control. Both types cater to diverse industrial applications, with HR-PIB gaining traction for its superior functionality in high-performance formulations.</p></p>

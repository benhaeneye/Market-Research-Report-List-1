<p><p>The elastic laminates market is categorized into three primary types based on elastomers. Ethylene-based elastomers are renowned for their outstanding flexibility and moisture resistance, making them ideal for applications like packaging and hygiene products. Propylene-based elastomers provide excellent heat resistance and durability, often used in automotive and industrial applications.

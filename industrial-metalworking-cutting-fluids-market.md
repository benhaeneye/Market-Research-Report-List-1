<p><p>The industrial metalworking cutting fluids market is segmented into four main types: </p><p>1. **Neat Oil Metal Cutting Fluids**: These are pure oils providing excellent lubrication, ideal for machining hard metals.</p><p>2. **Emulsion Metal Cutting Fluids**: These are mixtures of oil and water, offering good cooling and lubrication at a lower cost.</p><p>3. **Semi-Synthetic Metal Cutting Fluids**: Combining both types, they enhance cooling and lubrication while reducing oil content.</p><p>4. **Synthesis Metal Cutting Fluids**: These are fully synthetic formulations, offering superior performance with minimal environmental impact.</p></p>

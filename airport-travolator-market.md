<p><p>The airport travolator market consists of two main types: belt type and pallet type. Belt-type travolators feature a continuous moving belt that allows passengers to walk or stand while being transported. They are typically used for shorter distances and have a smoother surface. Pallet-type travolators, on the other hand, utilize flat platforms that accommodate larger loads and are designed for longer distances, making them ideal for luggage transport. Both types enhance passenger experience and improve airport efficiency.</p></p>

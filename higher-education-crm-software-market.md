<p><p>Higher Education CRM software is categorized into two main market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based CRM solutions are hosted on remote servers, allowing institutions to access and manage data via the internet. This model offers flexibility, scalability, and reduced IT infrastructure costs. Conversely, on-premises CRM software is installed locally on an institution's servers, providing greater control over data security and customization, but requiring significant investment in hardware and maintenance. Each type caters to different institutional needs and preferences.</p></p>

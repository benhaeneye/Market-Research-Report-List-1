<p><p>Transcutaneous Electrical Nerve Stimulators (TENS) are devices designed to alleviate pain through the application of low-voltage electrical currents to the skin. They are widely used for managing various conditions, including chronic pain, postoperative pain, and muscle rehabilitation.</p><p>The Transcutaneous Electrical Nerve Stimulators Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.2% during the forecast period, driven by increasing awareness about non-invasive pain management options and a rising prevalence of chronic pain conditions. The growing acceptance of TENS units in both clinical and home settings, along with an increase in sports injuries and rehabilitative needs, further supports market expansion. Additionally, research into the efficacy and safety of TENS therapy continues to validate its benefits, contributing to rising consumer and practitioner acceptance.

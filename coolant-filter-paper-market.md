<p><ul><li>Vacuum Coolant Filter Paper</li><li>Gravity Coolant Filter Paper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The coolant filter paper market comprises primarily two types: vacuum coolant filter paper and gravity coolant filter paper. Vacuum coolant filter paper utilizes suction to enhance the filtration process, effectively removing contaminants from coolants in various industrial applications. In contrast, gravity coolant filter paper relies on gravitational force, allowing coolant to pass through the paper naturally, making it ideal for simpler setups with lower filtration demands. Both types serve crucial roles in maintaining coolant quality and extending equipment lifespan in manufacturing environments.</p></p>

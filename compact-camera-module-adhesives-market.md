<p><p>The Compact Camera Module Adhesives market is divided into two main types: UV Curable and Non-UV Curable. UV Curable adhesives are rapidly cured using ultraviolet light, offering quick setting times and strong bonds, ideal for high-volume production environments. Non-UV Curable adhesives, on the other hand, may use heat or moisture for curing, providing versatility but often requiring longer setting periods. Both types are essential for ensuring the durability and performance of camera modules in various electronic devices.</p></p>

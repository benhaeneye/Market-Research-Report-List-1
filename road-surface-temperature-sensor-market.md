<p><p>The Road Surface Temperature Sensor market comprises two primary types: active and passive sensors. Active sensors utilize internal power sources to measure temperature, offering real-time monitoring and immediate data transmission. These are often employed in critical areas where precise temperature readings are essential for safety and maintenance. Passive sensors, on the other hand, rely on ambient energy and typically provide data based on environmental conditions. They are generally easier to install but may require periodic calibration for accuracy. Both types play crucial roles in transportation management and infrastructure maintenance.</p></p>

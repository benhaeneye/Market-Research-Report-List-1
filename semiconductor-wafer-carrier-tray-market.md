<p><p>The semiconductor wafer carrier tray market features two main types: in-process wafer carrier trays and shipment wafer carrier trays. In-process wafer carrier trays are designed for handling wafers during fabrication, ensuring protection and ease of transport within manufacturing facilities. Shipment wafer carrier trays, on the other hand, are specifically created for the safe transportation of completed wafers to customers or other facilities, minimizing movement and contamination risks. Both types play critical roles in the semiconductor manufacturing supply chain.</p></p>

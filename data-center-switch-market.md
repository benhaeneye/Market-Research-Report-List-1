<p><strong>The Data Center Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Core Switches</li><li>Distribution Switches</li><li>Access Switches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The data center switch market is categorized into three main types: core switches, distribution switches, and access switches. Core switches operate at the highest level, connecting multiple networks and managing large data flows with high bandwidth and low latency. Distribution switches manage data traffic between core and access layers, facilitating robust routing and load balancing. Access switches connect end devices like servers and storage to the network, enabling endpoint connectivity and local traffic management, forming the foundation of a data center's infrastructure.</p></p>

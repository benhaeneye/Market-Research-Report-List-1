<p><p>The pressure modulating valve market includes various types, primarily butterfly valves and ball valves. Butterfly valves utilize a disc to control fluid flow, offering quick operation and space efficiency, making them ideal for large volumes. Conversely, ball valves feature a spherical closure element, providing tight sealing and minimal pressure drop, suitable for on/off applications. Both types are crucial in industries like oil, gas, and water management, ensuring optimal pressure regulation and flow control in diverse systems.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Industrial Handheld 3D Scanner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laser</li><li>Structure Light</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Handheld 3D Scanner market primarily comprises two types: laser and structured light scanners. Laser scanners utilize laser beams to measure distances, capturing high-precision 3D data, making them suitable for complex surfaces and large objects. Structured light scanners project a series of light patterns onto the object, calculating depth through distortion analysis, ideal for smaller, intricate parts. Both technologies are essential for applications in manufacturing, quality control, and reverse engineering, providing accurate and efficient 3D modeling solutions.</p></p>

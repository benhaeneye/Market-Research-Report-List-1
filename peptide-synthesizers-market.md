<p><p>The peptide synthesizers market comprises various technology types: Solid-Phase Peptide Synthesis (SPPS), Solution-Phase Peptide Synthesis, and Hybrid & Recombinant Technologies. SPPS allows for efficient peptide assembly on solid supports, optimizing purification and yield. Solution-Phase Peptide Synthesis involves synthesizing peptides in liquid, offering flexibility but often requiring extensive purification steps. Hybrid technologies combine aspects of both methods for enhanced efficiency, while recombinant technology leverages biological systems for producing peptides, catering to complex structures and larger scales in pharmaceutical applications.</p></p>

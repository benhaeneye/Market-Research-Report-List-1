<p><ul><li>Nutritive Sweeteners</li><li>Non-nutritive Sweeteners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pharmaceutical sweeteners market is divided into two primary types: nutritive and non-nutritive sweeteners. Nutritive sweeteners, such as sugars and sugar alcohols, provide calories and can enhance the therapeutic appeal of medicated formulations by improving taste. Non-nutritive sweeteners, like aspartame and stevia, offer sweetness without significant calories, making them ideal for weight management and diabetic applications. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing patient compliance and overall product palatability in pharmaceuticals.</p></p>

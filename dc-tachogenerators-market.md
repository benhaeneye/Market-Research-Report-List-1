<p><ul><li>Permanent Magnet Type</li><li>Electromagnetic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The DC tachogenerator market is primarily divided into two types: permanent magnet and electromagnetic. Permanent magnet tachogenerators utilize strong magnetic fields created by permanent magnets to generate voltage proportional to rotational speed, offering high efficiency and compact design. In contrast, electromagnetic tachogenerators rely on electromagnetic coils and external magnetic fields, providing broader operational flexibility but often involving more complex construction. Both types serve essential roles in speed measurement and control applications across various industrial sectors, each catering to specific performance requirements.</p></p>

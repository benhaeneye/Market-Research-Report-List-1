<p><p>The Digital MEMS Microphone market can be segmented by interface types: I2S, TDM, and PDM. </p><p>I2S (Inter-IC Sound) is widely used for streaming audio data between integrated circuits, offering simplicity and low latency. TDM (Time Division Multiplexing) efficiently transmits multiple microphone signals over a single channel, optimizing bandwidth. PDM (Pulse Density Modulation) employs a high-frequency bitstream format, enabling compact designs and lower power consumption. Each interface type caters to specific application needs in consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial sectors.</p></p>

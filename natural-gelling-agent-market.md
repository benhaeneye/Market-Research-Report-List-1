<p><p>The natural gelling agent market encompasses various types that serve distinct applications in food, pharmaceuticals, and cosmetics. Xanthan gum, derived from fermentation, offers thickening and stabilizing properties. Gellan gum, obtained from bacteria, provides clarity and high gel strength. Guar gum, sourced from guar beans, acts as a thickener and stabilizer. Pectin, extracted from fruits, is widely used in jams and jellies for its gelling ability. Gelatin, derived from animal collagen, is essential in creating smooth textures in desserts and confections.</p></p>

<p><p>The Voltage Resonator Oscillator market includes various types such as the Wien Bridge Oscillator, RC Phase Shift Oscillator, and Hartley Oscillator. The Wien Bridge Oscillator is known for generating low-frequency sine waves with high stability. The RC Phase Shift Oscillator produces sine waves using resistive-capacitive networks, suitable for audio applications. The Hartley Oscillator utilizes inductors and capacitors for frequency determination, excelling in RF applications. Each type serves specific purposes across telecommunications, audio, and signal processing sectors.</p></p>

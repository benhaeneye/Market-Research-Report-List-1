<p><strong>The Non-Volatile Dual In-Line Memory Module (NVDIMM) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>NVDIMM-F</li><li>NVDIMM-N</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Non-Volatile Dual In-Line Memory Modules (NVDIMMs) are advanced memory solutions that combine DRAM with non-volatile memory, ensuring data retention during power loss. The market features two main types: NVDIMM-F and NVDIMM-N. NVDIMM-F utilizes a flash component for persistence, making it ideal for applications requiring high endurance and data integrity. In contrast, NVDIMM-N integrates DRAM with NAND flash and a power-loss protection mechanism, offering low-latency performance alongside non-volatile capabilities, catering to enterprise environments where reliability and speed are crucial.</p></p>

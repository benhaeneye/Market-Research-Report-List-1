<p><p>The glass lenses market encompasses several key types, including progressive, single vision, and bifocal lenses. Progressive lenses offer a seamless transition between multiple prescriptions for near and distant vision, catering to those with presbyopia. Single vision lenses provide a uniform prescription for one distance, suitable for those who require corrective vision for either near or far sight. Bifocal lenses feature two distinct optical powers, allowing users to see clearly at both near and far distances. Each type serves specific visual needs.</p></p>

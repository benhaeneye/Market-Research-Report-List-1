<p><strong>The Semi Rigid Re Seal Label Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Foiling Semi Rigid Re Seal Label</li><li>Cold Foiling Semi Rigid Re Seal Label</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semi-rigid re-seal label market is categorized into two primary types: hot foiling and cold foiling semi-rigid re-seal labels. Hot foiling involves applying a metallic foil to the label using heat and pressure, resulting in a shiny, durable finish that enhances aesthetics and brand visibility. In contrast, cold foiling employs a UV-curable adhesive to adhere foil without heat, allowing for intricate designs and quicker production times. Both types enhance product packaging by providing tamper-evidence and re-seal functionality.</p></p>

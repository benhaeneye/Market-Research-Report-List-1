<p><p>The desktop autoclaves market is categorized into four main types: steam, plasma, hot air, and others. Steam autoclaves utilize high-pressure steam for effective sterilization, ideal for medical and laboratory instruments. Plasma autoclaves use hydrogen peroxide gas in a low-temperature process, suitable for heat-sensitive items. Hot air autoclaves sterilize through dry heat, often used for glassware and metal tools. Other types may include specialized systems for unique applications, catering to diverse sterilization needs across various industries.</p></p>

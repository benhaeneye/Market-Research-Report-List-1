<p><ul><li>DLP</li><li>LCD</li><li>LCoS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D interactive projector market encompasses three main technology types: DLP (Digital Light Processing), LCD (Liquid Crystal Display), and LCoS (Liquid Crystal on Silicon). DLP projectors utilize micro-mirrors to produce images, offering sharp visuals and high contrast. LCD projectors employ liquid crystals to create bright and vibrant images, ideal for educational and business environments. LCoS combines the benefits of both technologies, providing excellent color accuracy and resolution. Each type serves diverse applications in entertainment, education, and professional settings.</p></p>

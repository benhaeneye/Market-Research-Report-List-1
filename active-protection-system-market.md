<p><strong>The Active Protection System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Soft Kill System</li><li>Hard Kill System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Active Protection System (APS) market is primarily categorized into two types: Soft Kill Systems and Hard Kill Systems. Soft Kill Systems employ countermeasures like electronic jamming or decoys to mislead and disrupt incoming threats, enhancing the survival rate of armored vehicles without physical engagement. In contrast, Hard Kill Systems actively intercept and destroy incoming projectiles using interceptors or kinetic kill methods. Both types play crucial roles in modern military defense strategies, offering complementary capabilities to improve combat effectiveness and survivability on the battlefield.</p></p>

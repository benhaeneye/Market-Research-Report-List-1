The market can be categorized into two main types: monochromatic and polychromatic confocal sensors. Monochromatic confocal sensors utilize a single wavelength of light, offering high precision and stability in measurements, making them ideal for applications requiring accuracy. In contrast, polychromatic confocal sensors employ multiple wavelengths, enabling them to capture a wider range of surfaces and enhance measurement versatility across various materials and conditions.</p></p>

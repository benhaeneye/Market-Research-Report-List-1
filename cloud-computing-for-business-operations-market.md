<p><p>Cloud computing for business operations encompasses several market types that streamline IT processes and enhance efficiency. </p><p>- **Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS)** provides virtualized computing resources over the internet, allowing businesses to scale their infrastructure on-demand.</p><p>- **Platform as a Service (PaaS)** offers a development platform for building, testing, and deploying applications without worrying about underlying hardware.</p><p>- **Software as a Service (SaaS)** delivers software applications over the internet, enhancing accessibility and reducing maintenance costs.</p><p>- **Recovery as a Service (RaaS)** ensures business continuity by providing backup and disaster recovery solutions in the cloud.</p></p>

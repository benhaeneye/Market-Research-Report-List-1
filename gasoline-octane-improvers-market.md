<p><p>The gasoline octane improvers market includes several key types that enhance fuel performance. Ethanol is a renewable alcohol that boosts octane and reduces emissions. Ethyl Tertiary-Butyl Ether (ETBE) combines ethanol with isobutylene for improved octane and lower toxicity. Methyl Tertiary-Butyl Ether (MTBE) is a synthetic ether that enhances octane but has environmental concerns. Tertiary-Amyl Methyl Ether (TAME) offers similar benefits with a lower environmental footprint. Other additives in this market include various proprietary blends designed to optimize gasoline performance and emissions.</p></p>

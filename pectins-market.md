<p><ul><li>High Methoxyl (HM) Pectin</li><li>Low Methoxyl (LMC) Pectin</li><li>Amidated Low Methoxyl (LMA) Pectin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pectin market is segmented into three main types: High Methoxyl (HM) Pectin, Low Methoxyl (LM) Pectin, and Amidated Low Methoxyl (LMA) Pectin. HM Pectin is widely used in jams and jellies due to its strong gelling properties at high sugar concentrations. LM Pectin requires less sugar and can gel in low-sugar or calcium-rich environments, making it suitable for reduced-calorie products. LMA Pectin, modified to enhance gelling under specific conditions, offers versatility in applications across food and pharmaceutical industries.</p></p>

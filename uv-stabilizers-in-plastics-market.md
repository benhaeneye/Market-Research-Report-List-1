Types include:</p><p>1. **HALS (Hindered Amine Light Stabilizers)** - These prevent degradation by scavenging free radicals.  </p><p>2. **UV Absorbers** - These chemicals absorb harmful UV radiation and convert it to heat.  </p><p>3. **Quenchers** - These act by dissipating energy from excited molecules to prevent damage.  </p><p>4. **Others** - This category includes various additives tailored for specific applications to provide UV protection.</p></p>

<p><strong>The Cranberry Powder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Freeze-dried Powder</li><li>Juice-pressed Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cranberry powder is primarily available in two main types: freeze-dried powder and juice-pressed powder. Freeze-dried powder is made by quickly freezing cranberries and then removing moisture, preserving nutrients and flavor, making it highly concentrated. Juice-pressed powder, on the other hand, involves extracting juice from cranberries, which is then dehydrated into a powder form, often resulting in a sweeter taste but potentially lower in fiber. Both types are popular for their health benefits and versatility in various food and beverage applications.</p></p>

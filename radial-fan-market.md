<p><strong>The Radial Fan Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Paddle Wheel</li><li>Open Wheel</li><li>Back Plate Wheel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radial fans can be categorized into three main types: paddle wheel, open wheel, and back plate wheel. Paddle wheel fans feature blades that extend from a central hub, providing high airflow and pressure. Open wheel fans have a more exposed design, allowing for increased efficiency and lower resistance; they are ideal for applications requiring high volumes of air. Back plate wheel fans incorporate a plate that supports the blades, enhancing stability and performance, making them suitable for various industrial applications.</p></p>
